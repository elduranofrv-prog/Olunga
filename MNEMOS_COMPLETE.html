THE MANIFESTO

Memory Is the Only Currency
That Never Devalues.

The internet was built to never forget. Every Billboard chart, every school newsletter scanned to PDF, every news broadcast archived on a server somewhere — the collective digital memory of humanity is vaster than any single mind can hold.

MNEMOS is the bridge between that archive and you. We deploy a cascade of intelligent agents — search agents, era classifiers, net-crawling memory harvesters, and emotion curators — to reconstruct the exact texture of the years you lived through.

We believe in the therapeutic power of remembering. The song that played during your school assembly. The phone everyone was passing around. The world event your teacher interrupted class to tell you about. These weren't small moments. They were the substrate of who you became.

We built MNEMOS so you can go back. Not in time — but in feeling.

HOW THE AGENTS WORK
01
Search Agent
You give it a year, a school, a place. It maps your query against the global net archive — identifying your temporal and geographic fingerprint.
02
Era Bracket Agent
Classifies your years into memory brackets: Early Childhood, Primary, Secondary, Young Adult. Each bracket gets its own memory excavation pass.
03
Net Memory Agent
The workhorse. Pulls Billboard charts, Wikipedia events, archived TV schedules, school records, YouTube upload dates, and viral internet moments from your exact years.
04
Emotion Curator
Synthesizes raw data into emotional resonance. It knows that a song doesn't just belong to a year — it belongs to a feeling. It frames your memories accordingly.
